Merri Creek House Publication

A 12-fold identity concertina for Merri Creek House by WOWOWA Architects, as part of the “Paper Architecture” brief. The extended format, meandering text and overflowing titles reflect the unique architecture and fluidity of the house. The book unfolds to reveal a collage poster on the inside comprised of images of the house itself.
