MPavilion 2015 Typeface & Identity

Typeface design and event identity for the 2015 MPavilion, as part of the “Typography 2” elective. The typeface is inspired by the thin stems and translucent petal canopy of the pavilion, focusing on constructing natural yet geometric forms. The event identity is for a proposed one-night music event to be held at MPavilion 2015 called “Canopies”.
