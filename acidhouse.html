Acid House Museum Identity

An all-in-one envelope, letterhead, business card and photographic concertina design for the Museum of ACID HOUSE. The identity references the peak of late 80s-90s rave scene, drawing inspiration from the psychedelia and playful darkness of the culture. All edges are perforated to allow for individual use.
